GlacialRailways

About

GlacialRailways is a 2D pixel art game where the player needs to build railways to connect diffrent buildings to make resources and sell them to make money. The game is still in early development but the goal is to make a fun and relaxing game where the player can build their own railway empire.

RailWays

The railway building is one of the core features of this game. This means it needs to feel smooth and work with no issues. After some brain storming and testing. We finaly came to the conclusion that a modification of A* pathfinding the best and smoothest way is to implement the railway building. The reason we chose grid based A* pathfinding is because its confined to a grid. This makes it so that the later deletion of tracks will be easier to implement. Also makes the intersections easier. because there will be a limited ammount of intersections.
